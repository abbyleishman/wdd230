Target Audience

The target audience for this website is new or recently moved business owners. SCV is a growing town full of opportunities for new business owners and a great place for well established businesses to expand. SCV is a very community centered town. There are many locally owned businesses, and as such, the town largely supports other new, local businesses. There are a large variety of people and interests so SCV is the perfect place for any new business.

Scenarios:

1. A man visits Chamber of Commerce to see the local business who he could work with.


2. A local businessd reached out to the Chamber of Commerce to see if they could be featured on the website.


3. A woman knows how Chamber of Commerce works and visits to see any good opportunity.


4. A mom wanted to check some information about local bussiness to choose which one would suit her better.


5. A man is looking for someone who could build a website for him so he looks on the Chamber of Commerce website.
