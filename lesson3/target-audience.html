Target Audience

Persona 1: John Appleseed

John has lived in the area his whole life. His family (his own and his parents) are well established and respected. He works as a doctor at his family's clinic and has a passion to help people.



Jim will use the site to:
  • View the community calendar for activties and events
  • View information on becoming a sponsor
  • Join the Chamber of Commerce/li>
  • Share resources with other businesses
  • View information on sponsoring businesses in the area

Persona 2: Jane Smith

Jane is new to the area. She moved to the area 6 months ago with her family. She is a mom to five kids, ages seventeen to four. She runs a side business through Etsy where she quilts custom blankets.



Jane will use the site to:
  • Look for other businesses similar to hers
  • Look at upcoming local events the Chamber of Commerce has planned
  • Join the Chamber of Commerce
  • Learn more about the area
  • Look at the directory to find other businesses to collaborate with
